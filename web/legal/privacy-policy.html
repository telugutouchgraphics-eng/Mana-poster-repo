Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This policy explains what user data Mana Poster collects, how it is used, and what choices are available to users.

What data we collect

  • Account details such as email, Firebase UID, and Google sign-in identity.
  • Poster profile details such as display name, WhatsApp number, business name, and uploaded profile photo or logo.
  • Purchase and subscription-related status needed to verify access.
  • Device notification token when notifications are enabled.

How the data is used

  • To sign users in and secure their account.
  • To personalize posters with saved profile details.
  • To verify subscription or paid-access eligibility.
  • To send optional notifications and app updates.

Third-party services

Mana Poster may use Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, Firebase Messaging, Google Sign-In, and Google Play Billing to deliver core app features.

User choices

  • Optional permissions such as notifications can be disabled from device settings.
  • Users can request account deletion from inside the app.
  • Privacy and terms information are also available inside the app help and legal sections.

Contact

For privacy or data questions, contact manaposter2026@gmail.com.
