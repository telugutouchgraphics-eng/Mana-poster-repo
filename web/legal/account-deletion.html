Account Deletion

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Mana Poster provides an in-app account deletion option.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the app and go to Profile.
  2. Open the account deletion option.
  3. Review the warning and confirm deletion.

What may be deleted

  • Login access for the account.
  • Poster profile details saved for that account.
  • Linked in-app identity data and active session data.

What may be retained

Some billing, anti-fraud, tax, compliance, or platform-required records may be retained for a limited period where legally required.
